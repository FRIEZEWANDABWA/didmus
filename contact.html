Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our campaign and policies

How can I schedule a meeting with Didmus Barasa?

You can request a meeting by calling our office at +254 700 000 000 or sending an email to info@didmusbarasa.co.ke. We'll work with you to find a suitable time based on the campaign schedule.

Where can I get campaign materials and manifestos?

Campaign materials are available at our headquarters in Bungoma Town. You can also download digital copies from our website or request them to be sent to your location.

How do I report issues in my community?

You can report community issues through our contact form, WhatsApp, or by visiting our office. We take all community concerns seriously and will work to address them appropriately.

Can I invite Didmus to speak at my event?

Yes! We welcome invitations to community events. Please send your request with event details, date, and location to our office, and we'll check availability in the campaign schedule.
