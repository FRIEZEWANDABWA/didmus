A County That Works for Everyone

Comprehensive policy agenda for transforming Bungoma County into a prosperous, inclusive, and digitally-empowered region where every citizen thrives.

Agriculture & Food Security

We will modernize farming with subsidized inputs, irrigation schemes, storage facilities, and market access, ensuring Bungoma becomes Kenya's breadbasket.

  • Subsidized farm inputs and equipment
  • Modern irrigation systems
  • Storage and processing facilities
  • Direct market linkages
  • Agricultural extension services

Youth Empowerment & Jobs

Establish digital hubs, vocational centers, and seed funding programs to create new opportunities for youth and innovators.

  • Digital innovation hubs
  • Vocational training centers
  • Youth startup funding
  • Skills development programs
  • Mentorship networks

Healthcare Access

Build and equip sub-county hospitals, ensure affordable medicines, and introduce telemedicine for remote areas.

  • Modern hospital facilities
  • Affordable medicine access
  • Telemedicine services
  • Community health programs
  • Maternal and child care

Education for the Future

Provide scholarships, e-learning platforms, and modern school facilities to give every child a chance to succeed.

  • Scholarship programs
  • E-learning platforms
  • Modern school facilities
  • Teacher training programs
  • Technical education

Good Governance

Launch open budgeting dashboards, digital citizen feedback systems, and regular town halls for accountability and inclusion.

  • Transparent budgeting
  • Digital feedback systems
  • Regular town halls
  • Anti-corruption measures
  • Citizen participation
